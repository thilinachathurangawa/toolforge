- [ ] Identify high-performing directories (most referral traffic)
- [ ] Consider paid upgrades on top-performing directories
- [ ] Research and add new directories to the list

---

## 10. Important Notes

1. **Quality Over Quantity:** Focus on high-DR, relevant directories first
2. **Natural Pace:** Submit to 5-8 directories per week, not all at once
3. **Badge Placement:** Add required badges to footer before submitting
4. **Follow Guidelines:** Read each directory's submission rules carefully
5. **Track Everything:** Maintain detailed records of all submissions
6. **Monitor Results:** Check Google Analytics for referral traffic monthly
7. **Update Listings:** Keep descriptions updated as new tools are added
